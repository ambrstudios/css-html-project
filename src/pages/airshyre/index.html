The Brig O' Doon

This bridge is iconic in Scottish folklore because of one of the greatest works of Robbie Burns, the Scottish Poet. His poem, Tam O' Shanter, recounts the tale of a foolish man who tries to outrun a ghoulish army to cross over the river Doon, as ghouls do not cross over water.

Having lived near his home and walked by the bridge itself, I can attest to the significance of the place, but the beauty is something that the photographer certainly brought to life.
